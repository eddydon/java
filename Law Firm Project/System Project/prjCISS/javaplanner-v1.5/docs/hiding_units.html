Hiding units in the time scale

Starting from version 1.5, you can hide specific units in the time scale (e.g. weekends).

To hide a unit in the time scale of a view, use the ignoreUnits() method.
As a parameter, the method takes a unit to hide (specified in the view's X-Axis units, e.g. a day in the Week view, an hour in the Timeline view). 

DHXPlanner planner = new DHXPlanner("./static/", DHXSkin.TERRACE);
 
//hides weekends in the Month view 
planner.views.getView(1).ignoreUnits(DHXDayOfWeek.SUNDAY);
planner.views.getView(1).ignoreUnits(DHXDayOfWeek.SATURDAY);
//hides non-working hours in the Timeline view (18.00 - 08.00 or 6 PM - 8 AM)
planner.views.getView(4).ignoreUnits(19, 23);
planner.views.getView(4).ignoreUnits(0, 7);

To show already hidden units, use the clearIgnore() method: 

//shows weekends in the Month view
DHXPlanner planner = new DHXPlanner("./static/", DHXSkin.TERRACE);
 
planner.views.getView(1).clearUnits(DHXDayOfWeek.SUNDAY);
planner.views.getView(1).clearUnits(DHXDayOfWeek.SATURDAY);
