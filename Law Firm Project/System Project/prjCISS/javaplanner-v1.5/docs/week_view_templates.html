 Week view

Week view


Available templates:

  • DHXPlanner.templates.setEventClass(String cssClass) - the css style for the event container.
  • DHXPlanner.templates.setEventHeader(String format) - the header of an event.
  • DHXPlanner.templates.setEventText(String text) - the text of an event.
  • DHXPlanner.templates.setHourScale(String format) - the items of the Y-Axis.
  • DHXPlanner.templates.week_date(String format) - the date in the header of the view.
  • DHXPlanner.templates.week_date_class(String className) - the css style for a day cell.
  • DHXPlanner.templates.week_scale_date(String format) - the date in the sub-header of the view.
