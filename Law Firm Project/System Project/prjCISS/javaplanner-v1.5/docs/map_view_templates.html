Map View Templates


Available templates:

  • DHXPlanner.templates.map_text(String text) - the text in the second column of the view.
  • DHXPlanner.templates.map_time(String date) - the date in the first column of the view.
  • DHXPlanner.templates.marker_date(String date) - the date of an event in the google maps popup marker.
  • DHXPlanner.templates.marker_text(String date) - the text of an event in the google maps popup marker.



Note, if the DHXPlanner.templates.setMapTime(String date) template isn't called, the 'd-m-y' part of the date in the google maps popup marker will be set according to DHXPlanner.templates.setDayDate() (2011-09-03 in the picture above).
